index_title: What Are Dashboards?
title: What Are Dashboards?
type: main
---
Dashboards are more than just generic overviews. Every Dashboard is built with a specific purpose in mind. They are based directly on your Insights and are designed to highlight your most important KPI’s and trends in one place. 

A Dashboard is built using two main components: Widgets and Tables. Both of these parts are highly interactive.
![image/size:large/align:center/frame:none](learn/images/155/e5cd411eabbe121df61669cab2747c3849e5e19bf8f2e4dc100341c27a41c544)

Before we dive deeper into the elements of a Dashboard, here is a helpful tip.

Dashboards can be customized to show many different overviews. To make sure you know exactly what you are looking at, simply click the "i" button. This reveals the specific Insights and data buckets that feed information directly into your Dashboard.

[id:2/topic: Widgets]

slide_id: b422d33e-da77-4d73-96e9-32fd5e609b81
index_title: Understanding Widgets
title: Understanding Widgets
type: main
layout: standard-2-col
---
Let's explore the individual parts of Dashboards. We will start with Widgets.

Widgets are designed to give you instant answers. On the top left, you will see the KPI. On the top right, you will see your target and whether you are hitting it. If you want month-per-month numbers, simply hover over the trend line.
![video/size:large/align:right/frame:none](https://pub-3c39fac4c588469d9bf40bf9e56bdbaf.r2.dev/Now-Dashboards/KPI.mp4)

[id:3/topic: Tables]

slide_id: 032dc1da-85ae-4324-aa69-70c6a4bab3d1
index_title: Understanding Tables
title: Understanding Tables
type: main
---
Right below the Widgets, you will find Tables. These display your KPIs based on different categorizations. Your data might be grouped by ABC categories or by the responsible buyer. Tables can also show individual products, like a list of your top ten best-selling items.
![image/size:large/align:center/frame:none](learn/images/154/39daef84716763d6973fc53bbc94ae1772f7bd68420e93faae6e112e4c356dd4)

index_title: Filtering on Categories
title: Filtering on Categories
type: main
---
If you click on any category in a table, it filters the entire Dashboard to show only that specific group. You will know a filter is active because the color of the Widgets change. 

In the graphic, the data is currently filtered to show only A items.
