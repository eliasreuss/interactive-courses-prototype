title: Why Supplier Performance Matters
layout: standard-2-col
type: main
---
Before we dive into the specific tools and strategies, let's establish why getting this right is so crucial to our success.

Suppliers play a critical role in the success of any company. While they are often viewed simply as providers of goods or services, suppliers are in reality key partners that influence a company’s financial performance, ability to innovate, and overall competitiveness. 

In many organizations, a large share of total spending goes to external suppliers. This means that the way a company manages its supplier base can have a direct and significant impact on profitability, operational performance, and long-term strategic growth.
