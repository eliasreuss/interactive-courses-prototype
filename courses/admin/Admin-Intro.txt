index_title: What Is Your Role?
title: What Is Your Role?
type: main
layout: standard-2-col
---
The quickest way to find out what your role is, is to look at your user color in the top navigation bar in Inact Now. Green means you are a User. Blue means you are a Super User. Purple means you are an Admin.

If you only see a Home tab, you are a Home Only User.
![image/size:400px/align:right/frame:none](admin-intro/Which-Role.png)
