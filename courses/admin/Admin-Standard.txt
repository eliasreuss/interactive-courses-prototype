[id:2/topic: The Four Important Tabs]

slide_id: 9ba9d651-8039-4737-b035-010dc54b1144
index_title: The Insights Tab
title: The Insights Tab
type: main
---
Let’s start by exploring the four most important tabs available to you, beginning with Insights.

Each solution includes around 30 ready-to-use Insights. Open the tab to see everything included in your solution.

Every Insight also shows its data source, so you can easily understand where the data comes from.

At the bottom of the list, you’ll find Private Insights, which are only visible to you.
![video/size:large/align:center/frame:none](https://pub-3c39fac4c588469d9bf40bf9e56bdbaf.r2.dev/Admin/Admin-Insights-Tab.mp4)

If you’re ever unsure what a column means or want to understand exactly what data you’re working with, head to the **Data tab.**

Here you can see all available columns, along with a description and a real example from your data.

Think of it as your go-to reference whenever something is unclear.
