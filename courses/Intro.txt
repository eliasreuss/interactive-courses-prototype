![image 1/size:third/align:center/frame:none](intro/Rising-Inventory-Capital.png) ![image 2/size:third/align:center/frame:none](intro/Too-Low-Turnover-Rate.png) ![image 3/size:third/align:center/frame:none](intro/Obsolete-Goods-And-Write-Offs.png)
