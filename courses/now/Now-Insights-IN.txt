index_title: What are Insights?
title: What are Insights?
type: main
layout: standard-2-col
---
Insights allow you to deeply analyze specific areas of your supply chain. You can think of them as highly interactive reports.

**You will encounter two main types of Insights in the platform:**

***Best-Practice Templates**
Inact Now comes with a large library of pre-built templates covering all of our solutions. These are built directly on industry best practices. This allows you to instantly apply proven analytical frameworks to your data without having to build anything from scratch.

***Custom Insights:**
Alongside our standard templates, you might also use tailored analyses built specifically by your company to match your unique workflows.

Regardless of how the Insight was created, it connects directly to your ERP system and updates automatically every day.

title: What Are Slicers and Filters?

---
Now that you know how to change **how** your data is displayed using views, let’s look at how to control **what** data is displayed.

**Slicers and filters** allow you to cut through the noise by segmenting your data based on broader parameters. You can slice and filter on almost any data point, meaning the specific options available will change depending on which insight you are using.

[id:4/topic: One Click Deeper]

slide_id: 7
index_title: One Click Deeper
title: One Click Deeper
type: main
---
Filtering your data is a great start, but true transparency requires going from broad categories down to the atomic level. We call this going one click deeper.

Let's say you are looking at your Double ABC Analysis. You want to see your most critical items, so you click on the AA category. You then sort the items by total cost of goods consumed to find the biggest contributor. From there, you click directly on the top item to dive into its individual performance. This process takes you from a broad category right down to the atomic level.
![video/size:large/align:center/frame:none](https://pub-3c39fac4c588469d9bf40bf9e56bdbaf.r2.dev/One-click-deeper.gif.mp4)
