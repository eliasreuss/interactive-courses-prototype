title: The Cost of Growth
type: main
layout: standard-2-col
---
As a business grows, its supply chain naturally becomes more complex. You have to manage more customers, a wider range of products, and an expanding network of suppliers.

For operational teams, this complexity often leads to lost overviews, reactive firefighting, and decisions based on gut-feeling rather than facts.
![image/size:550px/align:right/frame:none](learn/images/133/2023745b1b5d088bdae2e3d6b4685a7de6019ce7af49e9b8a027327d16380228)

index_title: Where Does The Data Come From?
title: Where Does The Data Come From?
type: main
---
The data in Inact Now is sourced directly from your company's ERP system and refreshed every night. Before going live, Inact worked closely with your company to define a scope - determining exactly which data is included and how it's structured. Understanding this setup helps you get the most out of the platform.
![image/size:large/align:center/frame:none](learn/images/134/6f894940ba8d6cf7419254a937f5688e0bdbaa956e2dd2bbda51cc7379db5da0)
