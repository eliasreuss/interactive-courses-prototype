index_title: What Are Actions?
title: What Are Actions?
type: main
layout: standard-2-col
---
You can think of actions as automated to-do lists. They continuously monitor your data in the background, and whenever something requires your attention, it is automatically added to your list. 

**This ensures you always know what to focus on.**
![image/size:550px/align:right/frame:none](now-actions/Action-Table.png)

slide_id: 3
index_title: Where Do Actions Come From?
title: Where Do Actions Come From?
type: main
layout: standard-2-col
---
**Each action list is based on filters defined by its creator.** 

For example, you or a colleague might create a list to help keep stock levels under control. In that case, you can set a rule so that any item within a specific category, such as CC items, that exceeds a chosen inventory value threshold, for example 100,000, appears on the list as a to-do item.

Think of it like a net that only catches the specific fish you are looking for.
![image/size:500px/align:right/frame:none](now-actions/Create-Action.png)

index_title: How Do I Access an Action?
title: How Do I Access an Action?
type: main
---
Getting to your Actions is incredibly simple.

Just like your Insights, your most important Actions are pinned in your Home screen for easy access. To open one, simply click on its name directly from your Home view.
