title: **What is on your home screen?**
type: main
---
When you log in to Inact Now, your Home tab will look something like the example on the right.

This page is built around three distinct sections: **Dashboards, Actions, and Insights.** Because the platform is tailored to your specific role, your view is personalized.

Depending on your specific tasks and what has been shared with you, you might see all three of these sections, just one or two, or none at all.
