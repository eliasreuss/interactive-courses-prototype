index_title: The hidden potential in your supply chain
title: **The hidden potential in your supply chain**
type: main
---
There are two main ways to improve your company’s profit margin: You can either raise prices on your products, or you can lower your costs.

Roughly 80% of a company’s costs usually sit in the supply chain. This means the potential for optimization here is massive.

However, many procurement teams lack transparency. They see the total spend, but miss the underlying trends and behaviors. This "blindness" means you are likely leaving money on the table every single day.

[id:3/topic: The "long tail"]

slide_id: 5
index_title: Identifying your "long tail" of spend
title: **Identifying your "long tail" of spend**
type: main
---
Let's look at a practical example. When you analyze "Who are we buying from?", you often discover a common trap: Supplier Fragmentation.

As companies grow, they naturally accumulate suppliers. You might find you are buying simple office supplies from 50 different vendors.

This is known as the "Long Tail." While it might seem harmless, this fragmentation dilutes your focus. You end up with a chaotic web of small transactions rather than a few strong, strategic partnerships.
![image/size:450px/align:right/frame:none](spend-analysis/Long-Tail.png)
