slide_id: 2
index_title: Why supplier performance matters
title: **Why supplier performance matters**
type: main
---
Before we dive into the specific tools and strategies, let's establish why getting this right is so crucial to our success.

Suppliers play a critical role in the success of any company. While they are often viewed simply as providers of goods or services, suppliers are in reality key partners that influence a company’s financial performance, ability to innovate, and overall competitiveness. 

In many organizations, a large share of total spending goes to external suppliers. This means that the way a company manages its supplier base can have a direct and significant impact on profitability, operational performance, and long-term strategic growth.

slide_id: 5
index_title: KPI 1: On-time delivery performance (OTD)
title: **KPI 1: On-time delivery performance (OTD) **
type: main
---
On-Time Delivery (OTD) measures how consistently suppliers deliver goods or services at the agreed time. It is one of the most important indicators of supplier performance because it directly affects a company’s ability to operate efficiently, meet customer expectations, and manage costs. 

With On-Time Delivery Performance in Inact Now you create a transparent and structured approach to supplier performance management and the root-cause analysis to improve it.

slide_id: 6
index_title: KPI 2: Procurement spend
title: **KPI 2: Procurement spend **
type: main
---
For most companies, 50–80% of total costs are related to the procurement spend. Spend analysis is the process of collecting, classifying, and analyzing a company’s purchasing data to understand how much money is spent, with which suppliers, and on what types of goods or services. 

With Inact Now you gain the insights to work strategically with your supplier portfolio, identify cost-saving opportunities and improve supplier negotiations.
