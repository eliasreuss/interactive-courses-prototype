slide_id: 4
index_title: OTD Inbound Confirmed
title: **1. OTD Inbound Confirmed**
type: main
---
**OTD inbound confirmed** tracks the percentage of orders delivered on time, based on the confirmed delivery date, over the past 12 months. It’s essential for maintaining strong service levels and reliable operations.

slide_id: 5
index_title: Spend and Inventory Value
title: **2 & 3. Spend and Inventory Value**
type: main
---
Next, we look at the financial footprint of the supplier:

-**Spend** is the total monetary value of goods purchased from the supplier.
-**Inventory value** is the amount of capital currently tied up in this supplier's goods in your stock.
![image/size:large/align:center/frame:none](supplier-performance/Spend-IV-Metric.png)
---
> [next] Continue -> 6

slide_id: 6
index_title: Inventory Turnover Ratio
title: **4. Inventory Turnover Ratio**
type: main
---
**Inventory turnover ratio** shows how efficiently you are moving a supplier's goods. It measures how many times the supplier’s inventory is sold and replaced over a 12-month period. A healthy ratio means capital isn't tied up unnecessarily. 

**Here is the calculation:**
![image/size:large/align:center/frame:none](supplier-performance/ITR-Calc.png)
![image/size:large/align:center/frame:none](supplier-performance/ITR-Metric.png)

index_title: Products A/B/C/Dead
title: **5. Products A/B/C/Dead**
type: main
---
Finally, we analyze the double ABC category distribution of the products you buy from this supplier. This metric shows how their products are spread across the double ABC categories, revealing whether the supplier mainly provides critical, high-impact items or slow-moving stock.

slide_id: 13
index_title: Drilling down to the products
title: **Drilling down to the products**
type: main
---
When you find a top supplier that is underperforming, you need to know exactly why. This is where the supplier framework and the inventory framework come together.

We drill down to the item level. Is the supplier's low turnover ratio caused by a massive amount of "dead" items sitting on your shelves? Or is their poor delivery score affecting your most critical A-items? Identifying the specific products gives you the exact leverage you need for your next vendor meeting.

Let’s take a look at the insight: Supplier Performance Benchmark - Products.
